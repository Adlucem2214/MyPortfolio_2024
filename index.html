EthioCal: Localized Clinical AI Health Ecosystem

EthioCal is a pioneering, localized, AI-powered health and nutritional tracking ecosystem tailored specifically for traditional Ethiopian cuisine. Most global fitness applications (e.g., MyFitnessPal) completely ignore East African diets, making it incredibly difficult for individuals—especially those managing chronic illnesses like diabetes, hypertension, and cardiovascular diseases—to track their daily nutritional intake.

EthioCal solves this by combining cutting-edge computer vision (3D volume estimation), a mobile-first native interface, and clinical medical guidelines (ADA, AHA, and Ethiopian NCD guidelines) to deliver localized wellness at scale.

YOLOv8 Seg. 3D Volume Estimation Flutter / Dart NestJS Backend NCD Guidelines

Ethiocal Admin Portal Console

The Ethiocal Admin Portal is a high-performance health and nutrition management console. As the structural backbone of the Ethiocal wellness platform, it empowers operational teams and clinical editors to monitor real-time user metrics, configure dynamic dietary restriction matrices, and manage localized bilingual medical content.

Leveraging React and TypeScript, the console interface simplifies intricate medical clinical rules—such as configuring user-specific micro/macronutrient threshold limits—providing secure control across the decentralized healthcare ecosystem.
